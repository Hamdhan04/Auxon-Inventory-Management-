Auxon Inventory Optimizer
How Optimizer Works

An RL agent manages inventory for multiple products in an e-commerce system. It must balance stock levels, demand fluctuations, and pricing decisions to maximize profit.

Goal

Maximize total profit across all products while avoiding stockouts and overstocking.
  • Optimal stock levels
  • Meet customer demand
  • Minimize holding costs

Challenge

Each step represents a single day.
  • Dynamic Demand (Spiky)
  • Overstock Fees
  • Stockout Penalties

Agent Strategy

restock → stabilize → do_nothing

Logics: If demand > stock, the agent triggers a restock to ensure availability.

Action Space

  • Restock: Increase inventory
  • Do Nothing: Maintain level
  • Price Adj: Influence demand

Reward Logic

Reward = Rev - (Hold + Stockout)

Agents learn by correlates actions to future rewards.
